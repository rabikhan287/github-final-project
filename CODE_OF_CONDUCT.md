# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience, education, or religion.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments
- Personal attacks

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Project maintainers will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:
1. Warning
2. Temporary ban
3. Permanent ban
